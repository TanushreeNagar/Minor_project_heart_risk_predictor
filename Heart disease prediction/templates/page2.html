Regression: Predicting continuos labels

Regression analysis is the statistical method to model a relationship between a dependent (target) and independent (predictor) variables with one or more independent variables. More specifically, Regression analysis helps us to understand how the value of the dependent variable is changing corresponding to an independent variable when other independent variables are held fixed. It predicts continuos/real values such as temperature,age , salary, price, etc. Example: Suppose there is a marketing company A, who does various advertisement every year and get sales on that.
